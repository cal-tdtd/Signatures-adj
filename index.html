  2. Open the hosted image or local preview page in a browser.
  3. Copy the rendered image, not the HTML source.
  4. Paste it into the signature editor in Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, or Superhuman.
  5. Send a test email to yourself and check desktop, mobile, reply, and forward views.
